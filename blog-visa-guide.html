What Type of Visa Do You Need in Dubai?

Dubai offers a wide range of visa options depending on your profession, goals, and duration of stay. Choosing the right one is the first step toward a successful life or business here.

1. Employment Visa

Issued by a sponsoring employer. Most common for those working for companies in Dubai. Valid for 2–3 years.

2. Investor / Partner Visa

For business owners and company shareholders. Requires company setup and minimum investment depending on the license type.

3. Freelance Visa

Ideal for creatives, media professionals, developers, consultants. Requires a freelance license and proof of expertise.

4. Golden Visa

10-year renewable visa for investors, entrepreneurs, scientists, and high-level professionals. Requires significant financial or professional contribution.

5. Dependent Visa

For spouses, children, and parents sponsored by a UAE resident (employed or investor). Requires proof of income and relationship.
